Don't Steal My Time - User Guide

This application is intended to help with time organization in any kind of meeting that can be splitted into different 'turns'.

The setup

The first thing you need to do is define your meeting time in minutes.

Alternatively, you can define an ending time (in local hour) for the meeting, and DSMT app will calculate the remaining minutes when you press start.

The second step is define the amount of time slots you want your time to be divided in.

You can easily add the right amount of turns by entering the number and pressing accept:

Or you can add them one by one with the add button at the bottom.

Each turn is represented by a card, it starts empty, but you can fill it with a representative label for that specific turn:

Now you have all you need to start your meeting, just press start

The Counterclock

At the time you press 'start' the counter will start with the first turn, showing a circular progress bar and a counterclock with the remaining time of the turn.

At the left you will have the remaining turns count to take in the meeting.

At the right you will have the current and next two turns incoming.

Below the clock you can see the overall progress for all turns of the meeting, with proper widths according to their respective durations.

The next button will allow you to jump into the next turn. The remaining turn's times will be redistributed automatically!

If one turn takes longer than expected, the progress bars will turn into red and the clock will show the remaining time to end the meeting.

When the meeting ends you can see the turns that has not been given any amount of time.
